RegWatch Demo Company Policy
Entity: FinNova Capital Services Pvt. Ltd.
Business Type: NBFC providing digital lending services in India
Policy Version: 2.8
Effective Date: 01-Apr-2025
Review Cycle: Quarterly
Policy Owner: Chief Compliance Officer

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: POLICY OBJECTIVE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This document establishes operating standards for end-to-end digital
lending operations, including customer onboarding, credit underwriting,
loan disbursal, collections, complaints, audit traceability, and
regulatory compliance controls. The objective is to ensure consistent,
safe, and legally compliant lending while balancing customer experience,
portfolio quality, and operational efficiency.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: APPLICABILITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 This policy applies to:
- Retail lending products originated through web and mobile channels.
- Partner-assisted digital lending journeys.
- Co-lending and sourcing arrangements where FinNova is lender of record.
- Internal teams: Product, Engineering, Operations, Risk, Compliance, Legal,
  Customer Support, Collections, and Internal Audit.

2.2 This policy does not apply to:
- Corporate term loans.
- Secured lending products managed by a separate credit team.
- Internal employee lending programs.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: CUSTOMER ELIGIBILITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Minimum Age: 21 years
3.2 Maximum Age at Loan Maturity: 60 years
3.3 Minimum monthly income threshold: INR 18,000
3.4 Eligible geographies: PAN-India except blacklisted pin codes.
3.5 High-risk occupations require enhanced manual review.
3.6 Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) are subject to enhanced due diligence.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: LOAN LIMITS AND PRODUCT STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Maximum loan amount currently supported: INR 5,00,000 per borrower.
4.2 Standard ticket-size bands:
- Band A: INR 10,000 to INR 50,000
- Band B: INR 50,001 to INR 2,00,000
- Band C: INR 2,00,001 to INR 5,00,000
4.3 Tenure range: 3 to 36 months.
4.4 Loans above INR 3,00,000 require additional manual approval from
    Senior Credit Analyst.
4.5 Repeat borrowers may receive an uplift of up to 20% based on repayment
    behavior and internal score.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: ONBOARDING AND KYC
----------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 Primary KYC workflow:
- PAN validation through approved KRA.
- Aadhaar OTP-based eKYC for identity verification.
- Name and DOB matching checks.

5.2 Address verification:
- Self-declared address accepted for loans up to INR 1,00,000.
- Utility bill or equivalent required above INR 1,00,000.

5.3 Bank account verification:
- Penny drop verification required for first-time borrowers.
- Name match threshold minimum: 80%.

5.4 Video KYC:
- Video KYC is optional and currently performed only for random quality
  checks and fraud investigations.
- No mandatory video KYC trigger exists in production workflow today.

5.5 KYC refresh:
- Triggered every 24 months or upon significant profile change.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: CREDIT ASSESSMENT AND UNDERWRITING
----------------------------------------------------------------------
6.1 Underwriting model inputs:
- Bureau score
- Internal behavioral score
- Banking transaction features
- Device and fraud risk signals
- Employment and income stability indicators

6.2 Rejection conditions:
- Fraud score above threshold
- Identity mismatch unresolved within 72 hours
- Adverse bureau tags (as per risk matrix)

6.3 Manual review queue:
- Loans above INR 2,50,000
- Bureau thin-file customers
- High device-risk profiles

6.4 Policy exceptions:
- Allowed under approved exception matrix and dual authorization.
- Exception logs retained for internal audit.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: PRICING, FEES, AND INTEREST
----------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 Standard annual interest rate: 24% (reducing balance equivalent).
7.2 Promotional offers may reduce effective rate subject to product
    committee approval.
7.3 Processing fee:
- Up to 2.5% of principal + taxes.
7.4 Penal charges:
- Late fee as per product schedule.
- Bounced payment fee where applicable.
7.5 APR disclosure is mandatory in Key Fact Statement.
7.6 Pricing changes require Compliance and Risk sign-off.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8: DISCLOSURES AND CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
8.1 Mandatory customer disclosures:
- Principal amount
- Tenure
- Interest rate
- Processing and other fees
- Repayment schedule
- Late payment consequences

8.2 Communication channels:
- Email
- SMS
- In-app notification

8.3 Language:
- English mandatory.
- Regional language support available for selected customer segments only.

8.4 Key Fact Statement:
- Shared before loan acceptance in digital journey.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9: LOAN AGREEMENT, DISBURSAL, AND REPAYMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
9.1 E-sign based agreement execution is mandatory.
9.2 Disbursal is made only to verified borrower-owned account.
9.3 NACH/e-mandate setup required where available.
9.4 Repayment reminders are sent at T-3, T-1, and due date.
9.5 Partial prepayment allowed with product-specific rules.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 10: COLLECTIONS AND CUSTOMER TREATMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
10.1 Collection outreach channels:
- Phone
- Email
- SMS
- App push

10.2 Collection conduct principles:
- No abusive language.
- No public shaming.
- Contact only within permitted hours.

10.3 Escalation bands:
- DPD 1-30: Soft reminder
- DPD 31-60: Structured follow-up
- DPD 61+: Legal and recovery workflow

10.4 Vulnerable customer handling:
- Offer restructuring request intake process.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 11: COMPLAINTS, GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL, AND SUPPORT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
11.1 Channels: in-app ticket, email, phone helpline.
11.2 TAT standards:
- Level 1 response: within 48 hours
- Level 2 resolution target: within 7 business days
11.3 Escalations above SLA are reported to Compliance weekly.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 12: FRAUD RISK MANAGEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
12.1 Device intelligence and velocity checks are mandatory.
12.2 High-risk triggers:
- SIM swap indicators
- Multiple accounts from same device fingerprint
- Synthetic identity suspicion
12.3 Fraud case management:
- Immediate block on suspicious disbursals
- Case triage with Fraud Control Unit
- Root cause reporting every month

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 13: DATA GOVERNANCE, PRIVACY, AND SECURITY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
13.1 Data minimization principle is applicable.
13.2 PII access is role-based and logged.
13.3 Encryption:
- Data at rest and in transit must be encrypted.
13.4 Customer consent records are retained for audit.
13.5 Vendor access is time-bound and monitored.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 14: THIRD-PARTY AND VENDOR MANAGEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
14.1 All critical vendors must undergo due diligence.
14.2 Contracts must include security, privacy, and confidentiality clauses.
14.3 Vendor performance and incident reviews are quarterly.
14.4 Outsourced operations require Compliance oversight.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 15: REGULATORY CHANGE MANAGEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
15.1 Compliance team tracks circulars from RBI, SEBI, GST Council,
     relevant ministries, and judicial updates.
15.2 Initial impact assessment is prepared within 3 business days.
15.3 Implementation plan is jointly owned by Product, Engineering, Legal,
     and Operations.
15.4 Regulatory change tracker is reviewed in weekly governance meeting.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 16: TRAINING AND AWARENESS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
16.1 Mandatory induction training for all customer-facing roles.
16.2 Quarterly refresher for compliance-critical teams.
16.3 Assessment pass threshold: 80%.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 17: INTERNAL CONTROLS AND AUDIT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
17.1 Maker-checker controls for policy and configuration changes.
17.2 Production pricing config changes require dual approval.
17.3 Internal audit performs control testing at least twice yearly.
17.4 Non-conformities require corrective action plans and closure evidence.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 18: RECORD RETENTION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
18.1 KYC records retained as per applicable regulatory retention standards.
18.2 Loan agreements, repayment records, and communication logs are retained
     for internal and external audit requirements.
18.3 Audit logs from critical AI decision points are retained for review.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 19: GOVERNANCE AND POLICY EXCEPTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
19.1 Any deviation from this policy requires documented approval.
19.2 Exception approval matrix:
- Tech Lead + Risk Lead for technical controls
- Compliance Lead + Legal Counsel for regulatory interpretations
- Business Head for commercial exceptions

----------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 20: KNOWN CURRENT LIMITATIONS (FOR DEMO PURPOSES)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
20.1 Loan cap in system currently configured to INR 5,00,000.
20.2 Mandatory video KYC workflow not enabled by default.
20.3 Pricing engine default still uses 24% annual cap.
20.4 Regional-language coverage is incomplete for all disclosures.
