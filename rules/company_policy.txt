Company Policy - Digital Lending (Demo)

1) Loan Limits
- Maximum loan amount allowed per borrower: Rs 5,00,000
- Loans above Rs 3,00,000 require manual approval

2) KYC Process
- Aadhaar OTP-based eKYC is used for onboarding
- No mandatory video KYC workflow is configured

3) Interest and Pricing
- Standard annual interest rate configured at 24%
- Risk-based pricing exceptions are not enabled

4) Compliance Operations
- Regulatory updates are reviewed weekly by compliance team
- Engineering changes are released in a bi-weekly sprint cycle

5) Customer Communication
- Key loan terms are shared via email and app notification
- Vernacular communication support is currently partial
