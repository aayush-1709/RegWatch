REGULATION A: RBI DIGITAL LENDING GUIDELINES AMENDMENT #3
======================================================================
Reference: RBI/2026/DL/AMND-03
Issue Date: 15-Mar-2026
Effective Date: 30-Jun-2026

1. Scope and Applicability
1.1 Applies to all NBFCs and regulated entities offering digital lending
    products directly or via Lending Service Providers (LSPs).
1.2 Applies to mobile-app-based, web-based, API-based, and embedded lending.

2. Loan Limits
2.1 Maximum permissible unsecured loan exposure per eligible retail borrower
    is increased to INR 10,00,000 subject to lender underwriting policy.
2.2 Internal system validation must enforce cap checks prior to sanction.
2.3 Any breach of limit controls must trigger hard stop and compliance alert.

3. KYC and Identity Controls
3.1 Mandatory video KYC is required for all sanction amounts above INR 2,00,000.
3.2 Video KYC must include:
- live agent interaction
- liveness checks
- face-to-ID match capture
- session evidence storage for audit
3.3 Lenders must maintain a verifiable chain of identity proof records.

4. Pricing and Interest Cap
4.1 Maximum annualized interest cap for covered products is reduced to 21%.
4.2 Pricing engines must block sanction if computed rate exceeds allowed cap.
4.3 Charges and fees must be transparently disclosed before borrower consent.

5. Customer Disclosure and Fair Practices
5.1 Key Fact Statement must include:
- principal
- tenure
- annualized interest rate
- all fees and charges
- total repayment amount
5.2 Borrowers must receive disclosures in accessible digital format.
5.3 No dark-pattern consent flow is permitted.

6. Data, Consent, and Auditability
6.1 Borrower consent must be explicit, granular, and logged.
6.2 Critical credit decisions must be traceable with timestamped logs.
6.3 Data sharing with third parties requires documented lawful basis.

7. Governance
7.1 Compliance owner must submit implementation readiness note to board/audit
    committee.
7.2 Internal audit must test controls within one quarter of effective date.

8. Non-Compliance Consequences
8.1 Monetary penalties and supervisory action may be initiated for persistent
    non-compliance.
8.2 Repeat breaches may lead to product-level restrictions.

REGULATION B: SEBI DISCLOSURE FRAMEWORK UPDATE (DEMO)
======================================================================
Reference: SEBI/CFD/2026/DISC-11
Issue Date: 22-Mar-2026
Effective Date: 01-Jul-2026

1. Material event disclosures must be filed within revised timelines.
2. Related-party transaction disclosures require enhanced granularity.
3. Board-approved governance exceptions must be documented and published.
4. Delay in disclosures may attract monetary and administrative penalties.

REGULATION C: GST DIGITAL COMPLIANCE ADVISORY (DEMO)
======================================================================
Reference: GSTC/ADV/2026/DF-04
Issue Date: 05-Apr-2026
Effective Date: 01-Aug-2026

1. Digital financial service invoicing metadata must follow updated schema.
2. Reconciliation cycles shortened for selected entities.
3. Reporting inconsistency thresholds tightened for high-volume institutions.
